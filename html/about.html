Our objective

We help you to express your personality and brand in a cool way. We supply useful letter-themed accessories such as pendants, key chains, golf pin, etc. to consumers who want to express themselves in an impressive and unique way, and businesses who want to improve their brand awareness. Unlike other shapes, letters are associated with unique meaning and sound. Letter-themed accessories let you give your friends and even yourself your clearer impression and message. We have been developing the know-how and the skill to turn letters including kanji into cool accessories.
